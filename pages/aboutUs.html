Our Mission

At Rate My Room, we empower the Tufts University community by providing a dedicated platform to navigate, evaluate, and enhance their housing experience. By facilitating honest reviews of on-campus dorms, streamlining the search for off-campus residences, and offering a marketplace for sustainable furniture exchange, we strive to ensure every Jumbo finds a place they can proudly call home. Rooted in collaboration, trust, and student-driven insights, Rate My Room serves the Tufts community.
